CRITICAL GAPS ANALYSIS
================================================================================

🔴 CRITICAL BLOCKER: Offline-First Architecture
   What's Missing:
   ❌ Service Worker (/public/sw.js)
   ❌ IndexedDB Integration
   ❌ Cache Storage Strategy
   ❌ Offline Sync Queue
   ❌ Offline Data Hydration
   
   Why It Matters:
   - PWA specification REQUIRES offline functionality
   - Safety app MUST work when networks go down
   - Users in emergencies need KB/contacts/reports offline
   - Cannot queue actions for later sync
   
   Impact: CANNOT DEPLOY AS PWA WITHOUT THIS
   
   What Needs to Be Built:
   1. Service Worker with cache strategy (3-4 days)
   2. IndexedDB schema and setup (3-4 days)
   3. Offline sync queue (2-3 days)
   4. Redux integration (1-2 days)
   5. UI indicators (1-2 days)
   
   Estimated Total: 2-3 weeks, 1-2 developers

   Impact: Not optimal on tablets (medium impact)
   Fix Time: 1 week
